About Us

About EasyGas Online

EasyGas Energy Solutions, was established in 2012, primarily to promote, distribute and retail LPG as the preferred energy source within Nigeria. We are committed to promoting this cleaner energy sources for use in business and hospitality establishments and homes because we firmly believe the future of energy in Nigeria is in gas, particularly LPG and because of the numerous benefits of LPG over the traditional energy sources e.g kerosene, firewood etc.

We currently own and operate an LPG Dispensing Skid Station in Ajah, and provide delivery services within Lagos Metropolis.

Onsite Refill

We can refill or top-up your cylinder for you at your location (limited to cylinders up to 12.5kg. Larger sizes will have to be taken away, refilled and returned)

Mobile Sales Unit

We have tricycles vans strategically located in Lagos, which will deliver and perform any of the above services alongside our other delivery vehicles.

Stop Gap

In the event of an unplanned outage, a filled cylinder will be temporarily delivered to the client, pending when their own cylinder can be filled and returned, usually within 24-48 hours. The client then pays for the gas used (per kg) and the full refill for their cylinder.

Direct Swap

Swap your empty cylinder with a filled cylinder (this is dependent on the suitability of your cylinder for a swap).
